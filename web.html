The Desktop Operation

Mobile is the ops center for today's global supply chain. An array of configurable posters displays crucial data tailored to a specific enterprise. Using the Elementum Transport, Exposure, and Perspective products to frame high-level information stakeholders can view real time insights to anticipate and reduce friction in their operation. As a design pattern, we position Situation Room as a broad view tool for the C-level. We are evoking the idea of generals in a war room watching troop movements and being able to circumvent risk.

The typical pattern across the Situation Room posters include: Oversized type for readability from a distance, dark interface to prevent screen "burn", open space and borderless copy, continuous motion and real time updates, and the ability to act with confidence if action is required.
