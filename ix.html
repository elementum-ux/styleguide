Our Design Principles

UX follows these four guiding priciples when it comes to design

Simple & Clean

Less is more and clutter is the enemy—so if it's not moving the user forward, it's just noise. When in doubt, cut it out!

Know Thy User

...And we are not thy user. We are relentless in research and qualified assumptions. We place our personas on a pedastal, so sayeth Elementum!

Empowering

Our products are designed to give you more time with the family, more accolades from the boss, and more peace-of-mind about your next move. They just work—with you and for you.

Personality

Nobody, and we mean nobody, does it like Elementum! We have a unique way of looking at Supply Chain Management and we're here to shake things up.
