Mobile

Management At Your Fingertips

This pattern gallery is for product managers, designers, and developers who are creating Elementum mobile applications. As we are defining and refining our mobile strategy, it can be a challenge to find examples of design best practices, especially for multiple operating systems. Whether you have been tasked with designing a simple iPhone app, Android app, or a mobile operating system to be named later, these patterns will provide solutions to common challenges.

The patterns below represent a white label collection of features that are typical across all of Elementum's current apps: Transport, Exposure, and Perspective as well as any future mobile apps.

 

LOG IN

Open space for individual app branding. Remember me check box for frictionless return visits. Password recovery link. Waitlist choreography for new releases.
